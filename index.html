Welcome to the Top Fifteen

The City of Stirling Top Fifteen Group represents excellence in bowling competition, bringing together the finest players from across the region since 2002.

About Our Competition

Our prestigious bowling competition has been a cornerstone of the Stirling community for over two decades. We bring together talented players, passionate supporters, and dedicated sponsors to create an unforgettable sporting experience.
