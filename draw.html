Competition Rules

Competition Rules
  • The competition will be run on a Knockout basis and under the W.B.B. rules.
  • The ties will be played on Thursday evenings in July with the Final on the second Sunday in August with a 2.00p.m. start. It is an individual toss.
  • The home team (team first on the draw sheet) are asked to arrange umpires and markers.
  • The competition will be a team contest, the determined by the highest number of points, two points for a win and one for a draw. Then shots.
  • If at the end of the game, teams are equal in points and shots the winner of the senior single will determine the winning team.
  • In the event of a team being unable to field a player/players, the opposing team will be awarded 2 points and 8 shots.
Local Conditions
  • If the tie is cancelled due to the weather and green unplayable clubs should rearrange the tie before the next round. Also inform the committee of this arrangement.
  • Teams are free to substitute players prior to the arranged tie.
  • All results of matches should be phoned/emailed to a committee member as soon as possible.
  • "The Final" Each club will host a final and then go to the bottom of the list.
  • You can go to www.scottishbowls.com click on to 1. Knockouts, 2. Stirling West District Nine. 3. City of Stirling Top Fifteen.
  • You can then register Free and info will be sent direct to you as it go's in.
